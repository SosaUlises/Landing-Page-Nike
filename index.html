NIKE, JUST DO IT

Our mission is what drives us to do everything possible to expand human potential. We do that by creating groundbreaking sport innovations, by making our products more sustainably, by building a creative and diverse global team and by making a positive impact in communities where we live and work.

We invest in the power of people to move the world forward. Sport redefines human potential, and when people believe in what's possible, they can break barriers in areas where we need change the most. Our work is focused on diversity, equity & inclusion; responsible sourcing; and building community.
